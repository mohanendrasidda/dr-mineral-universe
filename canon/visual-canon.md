## Dr. Mineral — character design (from existing renders)
- 3D, warm cinematic ("Pixar-style") render. Reddish-brown fur, cream/pale belly, large expressive intelligent eyes, small dark nose, oversized bushy tail curving upward behind him.
- **Signature prop: a single acorn**, usually held in both forepaws or in mouth.
- Reads believably at small scale (mascot/sprite) and as a hero portrait.
- **No costume, no lab coat, no glasses, no gadgets** — publicly he's just a squirrel; the intelligence is in eyes and posture, not props.

### Consistency anchor (paste into every generation)
> The same squirrel character across all images: warm 3D Pixar-style render, reddish-brown fur, cream/pale belly, large expressive intelligent eyes, a small dark nose, and an oversized bushy tail curving upward behind him. He holds a single acorn. Friendly, humble, curious expression. Consistent character, same proportions every time.

## ⚠️ Canon flag — the "chain" motif is OFF-CANON
Many existing renders wrap the squirrel in a literal **chain** (a blockchain visual pun built for the Lab Reward Token cohort page). **That motif violates "technology is invisible / never feels like a blockchain book."** A mascot tangled in a chain *is* the blockchain shouting.
- **Dr. Mineral universe assets:** use the clean **acorn/discovery** poses only.
- **Cohort landing page:** chained poses stay over there.
