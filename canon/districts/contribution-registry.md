# Contribution Registry

**Essence:** the district that remembers — where every contribution, from the smallest correction to the saving of the Seed, is honored and never lost. The *preserve / pass-on* heart of the civilization (and Book I's home).

## Who runs it
**Vellum**, an ancient tortoise, Keeper of the laboratory's memory (300+ years; never startled until the unsigned Seed). His caution is love, not stubbornness; he learns he is "only one link" too. Apprentice **Tally**, a young magpie — above ground magpies are mocked as thieves; her gift is the opposite of forgetting. Her childhood button-string gave the lab the *chain-of-trust remembering.* **Flaws:** Vellum is so slow that conversations finish before he answers; Tally talks faster than she thinks and once re-shelved a century of records by color.

## Walk in
The quietest hall: no benches, no arguing — only the long dim aisles and the soft endless ticking of the memory-machines, brass and glass and dark wood, always turning. Each wheel holds the *fact* of a contribution: that on such a day, someone noticed what no one else did, and it mattered, and they were the one. The smallest mouse's smallest correction turns its own small wheel, faithfully, long after the mouse is gone.

## What they make / do
They make *remembering itself* — and since Book I, they make it as a **chain**: each keeper their own honored record, each linked to the one before and after, so the many are kept *as* many, none blurred, none lost. The chain "guards its own truth" and now "keeps itself." (This is the in-world origin of the lab's invisible record network — born of love, never named as technology.)

## Secrets & open mysteries
- The restored chain runs back *past the founding* — one faint link older than the laboratory itself. Who was keeping and passing on contribution before there was a lab? (Book II horizon.)
- The deepest aisle still holds wheels no one has read in centuries.

## → Website feature
**The Hall of Contributors / contribution ledger visualization** — shimmering constellations of past work; new stars kindle as contributions land. The marquee feature for the dev/researcher audience: *see your work remembered, linked, unloseable.*

## Cinematic shots
The threaded chain of light running back into the dark; a new contributor's star kindling; Vellum reading the restored lineage with wet old eyes; the worn plinth-hollow of four hundred goodbyes (Crystal Vault tie-in).
