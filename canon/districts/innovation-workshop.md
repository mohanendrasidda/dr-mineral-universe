# Innovation Workshop

**Essence:** where ideas become prototypes — the loud, messy, joyful place that proves the lab doesn't just remember, it *makes.* The home of failure-as-data.

## Who runs it
**Juniper "June" Ringtail**, a raccoon, Master Tinkerer — dexterous black hands, endless curiosity, a workbench like a controlled explosion. Raccoons are famous problem-solvers and lock-pickers; June can build (or open) anything. **Flaw:** she starts six projects per idea and finishes maybe one; her half-built wonders colonize every surface. She and Dr. Mineral are kindred disasters — when they collaborate, *something* catches fire (usually fine, usually funny). She's the lab's comic engine and its bravest mind.

## Walk in
Sparks, scaffolding, the smell of hot metal and sawdust, the *clang-whirr-"aha!"* rhythm of making. Apprentices cause cheerful chaos; an assistant-bot misreads a blueprint and politely builds a chair upside down. On the far wall hangs the lab's proudest tradition: the **Wall of Noble Failures**, where things that *didn't* work are pinned with honor, because failure is how the next thing works.

## What they make / do
Everything's first form: the motion-woken lamps, drone prototypes, the early chain-machines (later perfected in the Foundry), the mining-squirrel rigs (handed to Robotics to refine). Show the *process* — debugging a misbehaving machine is a beloved craft here (the lab "listens" to a fault the way Dr. Mineral measured an acorn). A working prototype earns a small festival. The *build* and *discover* verbs at play.

## Secrets & open mysteries
- June keeps a locked drawer of "ideas too dangerous to finish yet."
- The acorn-key that opens the entrances was prototyped here generations ago — but the original notes are missing, and June *really* wants to know how it works.

## → Website feature
**Experiments in progress / whiteboards / engineering blueprints** — the "what we're building right now" surface, including the Wall of Noble Failures. Pure catnip for the dev/maker audience: watch real building, including the breakage.
