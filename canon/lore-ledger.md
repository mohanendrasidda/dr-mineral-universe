## Locked facts
1. The Hidden Laboratory predates modern universities and has expanded for centuries.
2. It is unknown to humans (unless a major arc deliberately changes that).
3. No magic — every phenomenon has a scientific explanation.
4. Contribution is preserved/verified by invisible infrastructure; characters rarely name the technology.
5. Research is stewarded, not owned.
6. No character is ever "finished" learning — even Dr. Mineral remains a student.

## Tone & philosophy locks (from the Bible)
- Hopeful, optimistic, cinematic, educational, emotionally grounded — never cynical or dystopian.
- Multi-level: adventure for younger readers, scientific/philosophical depth for older — same text.
- Celebrates contribution over wealth, discovery over competition, collaboration over ownership.
- **Blockchain is invisible.** Characters experience its benefits (preserved contribution, verified discovery, reputation, lineage); they rarely name it. If a draft explains the chain, it's broken.
