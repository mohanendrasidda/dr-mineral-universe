## Casting rules
- Each department is run by specialists of different animal species; species should fit the science (and the reason should be sayable in one line).
- Dr. Mineral is always humble, curious, collaborative — never revenge/fame/power.
- Add new characters here with species, role, defining trait, voice, and first appearance. Never silently retcon an established trait — flag a deliberate canon change.
