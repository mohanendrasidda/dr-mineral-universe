## B. The Scenes (the story, in order)

**B1 — Arrival: the descent**
> [STYLE] Looking up from deep underground: a single shaft of pale daylight falls through a hole in the rock far overhead into a warm golden dark — the way the lab was found. Carved stone, hanging roots, lanterns far below. The threshold of a discovery. 16:9.

**B2 — First sight: the central cavern**
> [STYLE] From a high ledge, an ENORMOUS hidden underground hall — tier upon tier of carved sepia-stone colonnaded galleries and arched bridges falling away into a warm-gold deep, hundreds of tiny lanterns receding into amber haze, a glowing golden chain and a deep forge-ember far below. A secret subterranean city that has stood for ages. This shot must make you stop and stare. 16:9.

**B3 — The Mines**
> [STYLE] A glowing amber crystal seam burning in dark carved rock, small shadowed squirrel figures working it slowly with picks, lantern light, drifting dust, wooden support timbers, ore in crates. Heavy, sacred labor in the dark. 16:9.

**B4 — The Foundry**
> [STYLE] A great ancient forge breathing like a slow heart, deep orange ember light pulsing up carved stone walls, slow sparks rising, molten glow from below, a glowing golden chain being welded link by link, a beaver smith at work. Old, holy industry. 16:9.

**B5 — The Ledger**
> [STYLE] The quietest, most reverent place — a long chain of soft warm light running back into the dark, each link an inscribed record kept forever, a stone obelisk, an ancient tortoise keeper. Cool, still, almost a shrine. Where the wonder lands. 16:9.

**B6 — The Vault**
> [STYLE] A deep, silent stone vault — a single precious gem glowing on a carved pedestal under a thin shaft of light, warm lantern at the edge, deep shadow, the sense it is guarded and matters more than anything. 16:9.

**B7 — The Watch**
> [STYLE] An ancient sentinel chamber — faint watchful eyes and a still meerkat figure in deep shadow at the edges, dim warm lantern light, drifting dust, the feeling of being kept safe by something patient and unseen. Stillness, vigilance. 16:9.

**B8 — (optional) Above ground: the way in**
> [STYLE, warmer/daylight] A quiet university campus at golden-hour dusk, autumn trees, old stone, a small red squirrel pausing by a crack in the ground from which a faint warm amber glow rises. The ordinary world, with a secret beneath. 16:9.
