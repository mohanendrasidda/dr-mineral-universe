# Per-District Environment Prompts (Nano Banana / Gemini)

11 interior scenes, one per gallery. Full painted scenes (no keying). **Save each to `assets/environments/<slug>.png`** — that's exactly where each district page's render slot looks. Generate 16:9 wide. After you drop them, I swap every dashed slot for the real art in one pass.

## Shared style + guardrails — PASTE AT THE TOP OF EVERY PROMPT
> Warm 3D Pixar/Disney-quality cinematic render, volumetric golden lamplight, deep atmospheric perspective, rich painterly detail, hopeful and wondrous (never dystopian), 16:9 wide, no text, no watermark, no UI, no logos, no blockchain symbols. An underground research city of intelligent animals, unknown to humans — **NO humans, no human figures or hands anywhere**; any creatures are small animals (squirrels, mice, birds) seen small or from behind, or leave the scene unpeopled.

### 1 → `assets/environments/contribution-registry.png`
INTERIOR — a vast hushed dim hall of long aisles lined floor-to-lost-ceiling with thousands of slowly turning brass-and-glass memory-machines, each a small glowing wheel. Cool blue-gold light. Overhead, faint threads of golden light link the machines into one great chain; a single new point of light kindles like a star. Reverent, beautiful, alive.

### 2 → `assets/environments/historical-archives.png`
INTERIOR — a warm dim honeycomb of small stone reading-cells, shelves sagging with centuries of journals, the air full of golden dust. Small floating archive-lifts drift between levels carrying papers. Honeyed lamplight, the dry-sweet glow of very old paper. Cozy, scholarly, ancient.

### 3 → `assets/environments/innovation-workshop.png`
INTERIOR — a loud joyful maker-hall full of scaffolding, workbenches, sparks and half-built machines. Warm forge-glow and worklight. On one wall a "wall of noble failures" — odd broken prototypes pinned with honor. Brass, wood, tools everywhere; one charming brass-and-wood assistant-robot mid-task. Messy, warm, inventive.

### 4 → `assets/environments/chain-foundry.png`
INTERIOR — a vast underground forge-hall, a great central reactor-forge pulsing like a glowing heart, throwing molten-gold light up the walls. A glowing chain of forged links runs through the hall, each link cooling from bright gold to dark. Heat-shimmer, sparks, rhythm, order under pressure. The engineered heart of the city.

### 5 → `assets/environments/sentinel-hall.png`
INTERIOR — a calm focused high-galleried watch-hall ringed with softly glowing watch-screens showing the city's vital signs (rivers, a glowing core, a chain). Clear sightlines into warm dark; small quiet patrol-drones drift; an owl or two perched, watching. Unhurried vigilance, never grim — warm gold light, deep blue shadows.

### 6 → `assets/environments/robotics-division.png`
INTERIOR — glowing crystal tunnels where a line of charming brass-and-wood mechanical mining-squirrel robots moves through luminous crystal veins; small journal-carrying drones drift overhead. Whirring machinery, warm sparks, veins of glowing crystal in dark rock. Wonder and motion.

### 7 → `assets/environments/the-commons-and-bakery.png`
INTERIOR — a warm crowded gathering hall: long worn wooden tables, mismatched chairs, steam and golden light, fresh bread glowing in an open bakery. Cozy, inviting, full of life — the heart of the city at mealtime. Empty of diners, or only a few tiny animal silhouettes.

### 8 → `assets/environments/ai-center.png`
INTERIOR — a cool dim quiet hall of softly glowing "thinking machines," rows of lamp-like minds that seem to be considering something. At its heart, a great glass water-chamber casts rippling aqua-gold light across the walls. Calm, contemplative, like a library that is awake. Cooler teal-gold palette than the warm halls.

### 9 → `assets/environments/educational-academy.png`
INTERIOR — warm classrooms carved in tiers of golden stone, soft light and floating chalk-dust, little desks and a great teaching space tall enough for an elephant. Hopeful, bright, the happy hush of learning. Empty, or with a few tiny animal-pupil silhouettes.

### 10 → `assets/environments/codeworks-loom-hall.png`
INTERIOR — a tall hall of great glowing looms, each weaving threads of luminous logic into bright moving tapestries that hang floor to ceiling. Threads of light run between looms like dependencies; one ancient loom weaves alone in a corner. Mesmerizing, structural — code made visible. Green-gold light.

### 11 → `assets/environments/version-history-museum.png`
INTERIOR — a spiralling gallery you walk backward through time, each alcove a preserved past era of the lab (older stone, older machines), cranes and scaffolding frozen mid-expansion. At the far end, a tiny five-room founding lab beside the vast present city. Warm, nostalgic, dim, lamplit.
