# Art Direction & Style Bible (v1)

The look of the universe — for every render, animation, and page. Warm Pixar-quality, hopeful, cinematic, never dystopian; technology glows but never shouts. Aligns with the shipped site (`site/index.html`) and the render pipeline (`07_asset-prompts-and-handoff.md`).

District glow accents vary by gallery (e.g. Registry `#9fc6e8`, Foundry `#f2a23e`, AI Center `#5aa8c8`) — use them as the local key light.

## Lighting rules
- **One warm gold key**, usually from upper-left; soft rim light to separate subject/tail from the dark.
- The world is lit by **thousands of small lamps**, never the sun (we're underground). Light pours *up* from below in the cavern.
- Volumetric glow, gentle falloff, deep atmospheric perspective. Darkness is warm, never cold or grim.
- A character render for keying: flat solid mid-grey `#777` or flat blue background (NOT green, NOT a scene).

## Material language
Warm dark stone · pale wood · aged brass · glowing crystal · gold lamplight · old paper · woven light (Codeworks) · molten gold (Foundry). Everything looks *kept and cared for*, hand-built over centuries — worn smooth, never sleek-corporate.

## Typography (shipped)
Cinzel (monumental display, e.g. LANTERNHOLD) · Cormorant Garamond (elegant headings/quotes) · IBM Plex Mono (labels/metadata) · Spectral (body).

## Color script — 8 beats (ordinary → wonder)
1. **Cold campus dawn** — thin blue-grey, low light, ordinary. `#1a2230`
2. **The acorn** — a single warm point in the cool. `#9a6a3a`
3. **The seam opens** — first gold bleeds into the dark. `#df9a3f`
4. **The descent** — warm air rising, lamps waking one by one. `#e7b765`
5. **First sight of the cavern** — overwhelming gold depth, breath caught. `#f6c878`
6. **The work** — busy warm life, many small glows. mixed accents
7. **The chain** — cool luminous thread through the warm (the one cool note). `#9fc6e8`
8. **Rest** — dusk gold, content, the lamp still lit. `#e8c06a`

## Do / Don't
- **Do** lead with warmth, curiosity, characters, and place. **Don't** make anything look like a corporate dashboard or a crypto ad.
- **Do** let technology glow as wonder (the reactor, the looms, the chain). **Don't** show logos, UI chrome, text, or literal blockchain symbols in-world.
- **Do** keep the squirrel an *ordinary* squirrel with an unusual spark — no lab coat, glasses, gadgets, or clothing.
- **Don't** use the off-canon chain-wrapped mascot poses. **Don't** go cynical, grimdark, or neon-cyberpunk.
- **Do** render characters on flat keyable backgrounds; environments as full scenes.

## Consistency anchor (every character render)
> The same squirrel character across all images: warm 3D Pixar-style render, reddish-brown fur, cream/pale belly, large expressive intelligent eyes, a small dark nose, and an oversized bushy tail curving upward behind him. He holds a single acorn. Friendly, humble, curious expression. Consistent character, same proportions every time.
