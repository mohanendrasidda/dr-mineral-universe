## Lore Ledger — facts future stories must not contradict
1. The Hidden Laboratory predates modern universities and has expanded for centuries.
2. It is unknown to humans (unless a major arc deliberately changes that).
3. No magic — every phenomenon has a scientific explanation.
4. Contribution is preserved/verified by invisible infrastructure; characters rarely name the technology.
5. Research is stewarded, not owned.
6. No character is ever "finished" learning.

## Visual Canon — Dr. Mineral (from existing renders)
- 3D, warm cinematic render style. Reddish-brown fur, cream belly, large expressive eyes, oversized bushy upright tail.
- **Signature prop: a single acorn**, usually held in both forepaws or mouth.
- Reads believably at small scale (mascot/sprite) and as a hero portrait.

### ⚠️ Canon flag — the "chain" motif
Many existing renders wrap the squirrel in a literal **chain** (a blockchain visual pun built for the Lab Reward Token cohort page). **That motif is OFF-CANON for the Dr. Mineral Universe** — it directly violates the "technology is invisible / never feels like a blockchain book" rule. A mascot tangled in a chain *is* the blockchain shouting.
- **For Dr. Mineral universe assets:** use the clean **acorn/discovery** poses only.
- **For the cohort landing page:** the chained poses stay over there.
- This split is the canon firewall doing its job — keep it.
