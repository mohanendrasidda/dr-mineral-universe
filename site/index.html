THE STORY

A squirrel, a lab, and a chain that remembers everyone

Above ground, Dr. Mineral is just a squirrel collecting acorns, and nobody looks twice. Below it, he founded a hidden laboratory built on one promise: that every contribution — every small good thing anyone does — is recorded, kept, and honored, so no one is ever forgotten.

The lab keeps that promise with a chain: a living ledger no single keeper holds, that guards its own truth. The squirrels mine it, forge it, and protect it — and the work never ends, because no one is ever finished learning.

WHAT WE'RE BUILDING RIGHT NOW

Beneath the story, this is a real blockchain lab

We're building a blockchain reward system — mining coins and tokens that reward real contribution: shipping code, finding bugs, advancing the work. Every contribution is recorded on the chain and honored, the same way the lab honors its own. The world you can explore is what we're building, made visible.
