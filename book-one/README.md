# Book I — *Dr. Mineral and the Unsigned Discovery*

**One thread of the universe — not the universe.** (See the v2 pivot in `../docs/06_universe-bible-v2.md`.) The product is the living world; Book I is a single, complete 16-chapter story set inside it. We keep it for its literary voice, but it is **subordinate to the world** and will be revised *against* v2, not the other way around.

## Status
- **Complete first draft:** `ch01…ch16.md` (~17k words). Arc: the unsigned Seed → the chain-of-trust remembering.
- Voice charter: `STYLE-AND-PRINCIPLES.md`. Locked decisions: `00_decisions-locked.md`. Synopsis/outline: `01_synopsis.md`, `02_chapter-outline.md`.

## v2 revision notes (when we do the prose pass)
Bring Book I into the living universe without losing its voice:
- **Lab as a civilization, not a place** — let the bustling world (districts, daily life, drones, trains, festivals, neighborhoods) live in the *background* of the mystery; widen beyond the four-district tour.
- **Flaws + humor** — apply Dr. Mineral's flaw sheet (`../canon/characters.md`); add comedy beats (misfired machines, apprentice chaos, the toast joke) to earn the emotional ones.
- **Show the engineering** — prototypes, debugging, the joy of making, on-stage.
- **Broadened mission** — the Contribution Registry is one department; nod to the wider civilization (discover/build/protect), so the book sets up the universe, not just one mystery.
- **Hooks to explorable places** — scenes should make readers want to *walk into* the districts the website will let them explore.

Build to canon: `../docs/06_universe-bible-v2.md` (authoritative on scope) + `../docs/01_universe-bible-v1.md` (the soul) + the `../canon/` registry.
